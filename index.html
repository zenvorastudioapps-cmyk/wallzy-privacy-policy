1. Overview

Wallzy is a wallpaper application that allows users to browse, preview, save, favorite, download, and apply static and live wallpapers. The app may use internet connectivity to load wallpaper images, thumbnails, videos, configuration data, and related content.

We do not require users to create an account to use Wallzy. We do not ask users to provide their name, phone number, address, payment information, or account credentials inside the app.

The information disclosed in this Privacy Policy should match the information declared in Google Play Console’s Data Safety section for Wallzy.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

Wallzy does not require users to submit personal information directly inside the app. If you contact us by email, we may receive your email address and any information you choose to include in your message.

Information stored locally on your device

Wallzy may store certain information locally on your device to provide app features and improve performance. This may include:

  • Favorite wallpapers selected by the user.
  • Recently viewed or cached wallpaper data.
  • Search history or recent searches, if this feature is enabled in the app.
  • App preferences and settings.
  • Cached thumbnails, images, videos, or temporary media files.

Technical and usage-related information

Depending on the app version, configuration, and third-party services used, limited technical information may be processed by service providers. This may include:

  • Device type and operating system version.
  • App version and basic app configuration data.
  • Crash, diagnostic, or performance information if crash reporting or analytics services are enabled.
  • Network-related information required to load remote wallpaper content.

Wallzy does not intentionally collect sensitive personal information such as government identification numbers, financial information, health information, contacts, SMS, call logs, precise location, microphone recordings, or camera images.

3. How We Use Information

Information handled by Wallzy is used for the following purposes:

  • To display wallpapers, thumbnails, categories, and live wallpaper previews.
  • To allow users to save favorites and app preferences.
  • To improve loading speed through local caching.
  • To download or apply wallpapers when requested by the user.
  • To configure app content or behavior through remote configuration, if used.
  • To maintain app stability, security, and performance.
  • To respond to user support requests sent by email.

4. App Permissions

Wallzy may request certain Android permissions depending on your device version and the features you use.

  • Internet access: Used to load wallpapers, thumbnails, live wallpaper media, configuration data, and related online content.
  • Storage or media access: May be used on supported Android versions when saving or downloading wallpapers to your device.
  • Set wallpaper access: Used when you choose to apply a wallpaper through the app.
  • Notifications: If used in a future or current app version, notifications may be requested only for app-related updates or user-facing features.

Permission availability and wording may vary depending on Android version, device manufacturer, and app version. You can manage app permissions at any time through your device settings.

5. Wallpaper Content

Wallzy loads wallpapers, thumbnails, live wallpaper videos, and related media from remote sources or content servers. Internet access is required to browse, preview, and download online wallpaper content.

Some wallpaper content may be cached temporarily on your device to improve performance and reduce repeated loading.

6. Local Storage and Cache

Wallzy may store app data locally on your device. Local data may include favorites, settings, cached thumbnails, cached wallpaper media, and search-related data if supported by the app.

Local data is generally stored on your device and is used to improve app performance and user experience. You can remove locally stored data by clearing the app’s storage/cache from your device settings or uninstalling the app.

7. Third-Party Services

Wallzy may use trusted third-party services, SDKs, or platform services to operate and improve the app. These services may process limited technical information according to their own privacy policies.

Third-party services may include, depending on the app version:

  • Google Play Services
  • Firebase Remote Config
  • Firebase or Google services used for app configuration, diagnostics, or performance, if enabled
  • Remote storage, hosting, or content delivery services used to provide wallpaper media

Third-party providers may collect technical data such as device information, app version, crash logs, diagnostics, identifiers, or network information when required to provide their services. Their handling of information is governed by their own privacy policies and terms.

8. Data Sharing

Zenvora Studio does not sell users’ personal information.

We may share or allow processing of limited information only in the following cases:

  • With service providers that help operate app features, remote configuration, hosting, content delivery, diagnostics, or app performance.
  • When required to comply with applicable laws, regulations, legal processes, or enforceable governmental requests.
  • To protect the rights, safety, security, or integrity of users, the app, Zenvora Studio, or others.

Any data sharing or processing by third-party SDKs used in Wallzy should also be accurately reflected in the app’s Google Play Data Safety section.

9. Data Retention and Deletion

Local app data

Locally stored app data, such as favorites, settings, cached files, and search history if enabled, remains on your device until it is deleted by you, cleared by the app, removed through device settings, or removed when the app is uninstalled.

Support emails

If you contact us by email, we may retain your message and email address for as long as reasonably necessary to respond to your request, maintain support records, resolve disputes, or comply with legal obligations.

Deletion requests

Wallzy does not currently require user account creation. If you want to request deletion of information you have provided to us by email, or if you have questions about local app data, contact us at: zenvorastudioapps@gmail.com.

You can also delete local app data directly from your device by clearing Wallzy’s app storage/cache or uninstalling the app.

10. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to help protect information handled by Wallzy. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Users should download Wallzy only from trusted app stores and keep their device operating system updated for better security.
